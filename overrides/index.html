About MACH

MACH (Metropolitan Aerospace Combustion Hub) is a student-led group dedicated to aerospace innovation. We design and build flight computers, ground station software, and conduct both hot-fire and cold-flow rocket tests—producing technical reports that advance our field.

Our projects span from circuit design for avionics, data acquisition during engine tests, to mission control software for high-altitude flights. MACH provides hands-on aerospace experience for students passionate about propulsion and avionics.
