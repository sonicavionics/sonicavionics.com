About Sonic Avionics

Sonic Avionics develops open-source, modular avionics platforms for students and rocketry enthusiasts. Our 4-inch scalable flight computers, compatible with the Mojave Sphinx, bridge the gap between theory and practice, addressing the engineering skills shortage through hands-on learning.

All hardware and code is freely available on GitHub, and we actively encourage community feedback and contributions. Join our Discord to discuss development and share ideas!
